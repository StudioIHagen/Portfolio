ABOUT

Jonas Hagen Jensen is a Norwegian architecture student at The Oslo school of Architecture and Design, with exchange studies completed at Politecnico di Milano. His work focuses on adaptive reuse, bioclimatic design and architectural representation through digital modelling and visualisation.
Alongside his studies, he has served as NALs student representative at AHO and worked as security for the National Museum of Norway.
Expected graduation: January 2028.
